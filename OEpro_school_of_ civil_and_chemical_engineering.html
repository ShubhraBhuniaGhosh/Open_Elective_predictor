CE1407 Heat Transfer Operations [L T P C – 3 0 0 3]

Pre-requisite: CE1305 and ES1103

Heat conduction, Molecular diffusion, Convective heat transfer (laminar & turbulent), Heat exchanger design: Double-pipe / Shell-and-tube, compact exchangers. Boiling: pool and convective boiling, correlations, design of reboilers, Condensation: film-wise and drop-wise condensation, correlations, design of condensers. Radiation: thermal radiation, radiation properties, view factors, heat exchange between surfaces. Evaporators: type of equipment, single and multiple effect evaporators, Crystallization: phase equilibria, crystal growth, types of equipment, design. (Use of Aspen/MATLAB wherever possible)

3. Holman, J. P., Heat Transfer, 10th ed., McGraw Hill, New York, 2002.

CV1590 ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT ASSESMENT [3 0 0 3]

Prediction and Assessments of Impacts: Impacts on air, water, biota, noise, cultural and socio-economic environment.
